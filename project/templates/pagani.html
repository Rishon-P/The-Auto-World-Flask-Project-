Pagani Automobili

  • Pagani Automobili S.p.A. (commonly known as Pagani) is an Italian manufacturer specializing in high-performance hypercars and advanced carbon fiber components.
  • Founded in 1992 by Argentine-Italian engineer and entrepreneur Horacio Pagani, the company is headquartered in San Cesario sul Panaro, near Modena, Italy.
  • The brand gained global recognition with its debut model, the Pagani Zonda, followed by the critically acclaimed Pagani Huayra and the latest Pagani Utopia.
  • Pagani's cars often feature a V12 engine developed in collaboration with Mercedes-AMG . Pagani sometimes involves their customers in the development and testing of new models, allowing them to experience their cars on the track.
  • Pagani vehicles, particularly models like the Huayra R Evo and the Zonda R, are often seen at events like the "Arte in Pista" program, which focuses on track driving and performance. They have also been featured at car shows like the Auto e Moto d'Epoca in Bologna and the Goodwood Festival of Speed. These events showcase their high-performance capabilities and design.
