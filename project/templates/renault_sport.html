Renault Sport Racing

  • Renault Sport (French pronunciation: [ʁəno spɔʁ]) or Renaultsport, was a motorsport, performance and special vehicles division for Renault-badged cars and later a sub-badge of Renault cars managed by Alpine.
  • The first Renault Sport was officially established as a company in 1976 as a merger between the Alpine and Gordini competition departments. It was made a division and reorganised in 2002 and 2016.
  • In December 2021, all Renault Sport operations were merged into an Alpine-led business unit.[3] The Renault Sport car range under Alpine was completely phased out by the end of 2023
  • Renault Sport (now Alpine F1 Team) participates in Formula 1 (F1). Additionally, Renault Sport has been involved in various other racing series, including Formula E (FE) and the Renault Sport Trophy. They also have a presence in eSports.
  • The European Rally Championship was won three times by a Renault car (1999, 2004, 2005).
  • The Renault Sport Racing was closed in December 2023 .
