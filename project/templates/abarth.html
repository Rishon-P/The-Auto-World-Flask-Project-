Abarth

  • Abarth & C. S.p.A. (pronounced [ˈaːbart]) is an Italian racing- and road-car maker and performance division founded by Italo-Austrian Carlo Abarth in 1949
  • Abarth & C. S.p.A. is owned by Stellantis through its Italian subsidiary. Abarth's logo is a shield with a stylized scorpion on a yellow and red background, a short, wide Italian flag in the middle, and "Abarth" text on a black background.
  • Abarth, funded by Armando Scagliarini,[a] took over Cisitalia's assets and on 31 March 1949, Abarth & C. was founded in Bologna. Carlo's astrological sign, Scorpio, was chosen as the company logo.
  • Abarth's motorsport involvement spans several racing series. They are particularly known for their presence in the FIA European Rally Championship, where the Abarth Rally Cup is a major focus. Additionally, Abarth powers and supports the Italian and German F4 Championships. They also have a history of supporting various rally events with their Abarth 124 Rally car.
  • Abarth has a partnership with the Movistar Yamaha MotoGP team for motorcycle racing .
