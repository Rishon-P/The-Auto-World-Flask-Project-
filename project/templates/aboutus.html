About us

Welcome to the AutoWorld !!!!!!! The main objective is to educate the young automobile passionates about a few global automobile companies . They can browse for companies based on the company's name or the country from which they originate . We also provide details about top 5 largest car races in the world .
Basically , you can view companies based on the countries they originate from , you need to select the option "Go by countries" . You will be asked to select a continent . Later , you have to select a country after which you will be asked to select the companies originating from that particular country . After the selecting a particular by clicking on their logo, you will be given the a few important detail about that particular company .
You can select the "learn about different races" to get details on the world's top 5 largest car races .
